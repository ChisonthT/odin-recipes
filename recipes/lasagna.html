Lasagna

Description

Layered italien ingredients such as tomato, pasta, ground beef,and spinach. If you stack two lasagnas together you end up with one lasagna.

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef (or another protein of your choice)
  • Spinach
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Lasagna Pasta
  1. Cook the protein halfway.
  2. Mix the cooked spinach and cottage cheese to make a spread.
  3. Layer the pasta with the tomato sauce, spinach spread and protein while seperating each layer with a layer of pasta and cheese. Repeat this step until you have a satisfactory height for your lasagna.
  4. Add a final layer of tomato sauce and cheese on top.
  5. Bake until cooked, take it out after its done cooking and before it starts overcooking.
